I am a postdoctoral fellow in Karin Kettenring's lab at Utah State University studying social-ecological management of invasive species. I completed my PhD in Andrea Kramer's lab, part of the Program in Plant Biology and Conservation offered jointly by Northwestern University and Chicago Botanic Garden.

I am a plant community ecologist interested in restoration ecology and invasion biology. My dissertation research tested community assembly theory in restorations by investigating how characteristics of native species influence invasion.
